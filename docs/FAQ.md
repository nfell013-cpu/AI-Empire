# ❓ AI Empire – Frequently Asked Questions

---

## General

### What is AI Empire?
AI Empire is an all-in-one AI platform offering **45 specialized AI tools** for developers, marketers, creators, students, business owners, and more. Each tool is powered by advanced AI to help you work smarter and faster.

### Do I need to install anything?
No! AI Empire is a **web-based platform**. Just open your browser, sign up, and start using any tool immediately.

### Is there a free plan?
Yes! Every user gets:
- **1 free scan per tool** (no credit card needed)
- The ability to **earn tokens by watching ads**
- Access to basic AI analysis

## Account & Login

### How do I create an account?
Visit [aiempire-one.vercel.app](https://aiempire-one.vercel.app), click "Sign Up", and enter your email and password.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions to reset your password.

### Can I change my email address?
Visit your **Profile** page from the dashboard to update your account details.

## Tokens

### What are tokens?
Tokens are the in-app currency for AI Empire. You can use tokens to access AI tools without a subscription.

### How do I earn free tokens?
Click the **"Earn Tokens"** button in the sidebar and watch a short ad. Tokens are credited instantly.

### How many tokens does each tool cost?
Token costs vary by tool tier:
- **Basic tools**: 1 token per use
- **Standard tools**: 2 tokens per use
- **Premium tools**: 3 tokens per use
- **Enterprise tools**: 5 tokens per use

### Can I buy tokens?
Yes! Token packs range from 100 tokens ($4.99) to 10,000 tokens ($249.99).

## Advertising

### Can I advertise on AI Empire?
Yes! Visit the **Advertise** page to submit your ad. We support video, image, and text ads.

### How does ad pricing work?
Ad pricing is based on:
- **Ad duration** (longer ads cost more)
- **Target applications** (single app vs. all apps)
- **Ad type** (video > image > text)

### How do users interact with my ad?
Users voluntarily watch ads to earn tokens. This means your audience is actively engaged and paying attention.
