# 🚀 AI Empire – Quick Start Guide

Welcome to **AI Empire**, your all-in-one platform with **45 powerful AI tools**!

---

## Getting Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Visit [aiempire-one.vercel.app](https://aiempire-one.vercel.app)
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. You're in! 🎉

### Step 2: Try Your First AI Tool (Free!)
Every new user gets **1 free scan per tool** — no credit card required.

1. From your **Dashboard**, browse the available AI tools
2. Click on any tool (e.g., **CodeAudit**, **DocuWise**, or **MemeMint**)
3. Enter your data or upload a file
4. Click **"Analyze"** and watch the AI work its magic

### Step 3: Choose Your Plan
When you're ready for more, pick a plan that fits:

| Plan | Price | What You Get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Free** | $0/forever | 1 free scan per tool, earn tokens by watching ads |
| **Basic** | $29.99/mo | Access to 10 AI apps |
| **Pro** | $59.99/mo | Access to 25 AI apps |
| **Ultimate** | $99.99/mo | Access to ALL 45 AI apps |
| **Individual App** | $9.99/mo | Subscribe to any single tool |

## 💰 Earning Free Tokens

You can earn tokens **for free** by watching short ads:

1. Click the **"Earn Tokens"** button in the sidebar
2. Watch a short ad (15–60 seconds)
3. Tokens are automatically credited to your balance
4. Use tokens to access AI tools without a subscription
